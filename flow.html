Fixed

→ Now: 📅 Due: 14 Apr 2026 6:00 PM
SETTINGS = {"RELATIVE_BASE": datetime.now()} evaluated ONCE at module load. dateparser.parse("6pm", settings=stale_SETTINGS) returned None, so time fell back to 12:00.

Open
Anjali cake 14 Apr at 3
📅 Due: 14 Apr 3:00 PM (assumed PM — may be wrong)
→ Should ask: "3am or 3pm?" when ambiguous
PM inference is applied for hours 1–7 ("at 3" → "at 3pm"). But 8am appointments (e.g. "at 8") are left ambiguous and may default to PM.
🔴 Open: Confirm step now shows time — vendor can catch this before saving.
